Train Trip Journal

Riding Amtrak from Boston to LA

Ride 1: Boston to New York

November 14, 2025

Saturday in Brooklyn

On Friday, November 14th, I began my trip. I used the first of 10 rides on my Amtrak USA Rail pass to take the Northeast Regional, train 179, from Boston to New York, leaving at 7:47pm and arriving at 11:49pm. My train to Chicago, the Lake Shore Limited, normally has a branch that operates out of Boston, but that branch is closed for maintenance, so I had to catch it from New York. Fortunately for me, Meha was kind enough to take me in for the night. We got to spend most of Saturday hanging out around Brooklyn.
